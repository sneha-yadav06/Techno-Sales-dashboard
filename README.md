# 📊 Techno Sales Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive **Techno Sales Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze sales performance, product categories, brands, supervisors, and regional sales trends for **ABC Company (2022–2024)**. This project demonstrates **Power BI, DAX, Power Query, data modeling, and business intelligence** skills through an interactive and visually appealing dashboard.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The **Techno Sales Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of the company's sales performance between **2022 and 2024**. It enables business managers and decision-makers to monitor sales, costs, profits, product demand, supervisor performance, and geographical sales distribution.

The dashboard converts raw sales data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations, helping organizations make data-driven business decisions.

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Monitor product category-wise quantity sold.
- Compare sales across different brands.
- Analyze state-wise sales distribution.
- Track supervisor-wise performance.
- Monitor overall business KPIs.
- Identify high-performing products and brands.
